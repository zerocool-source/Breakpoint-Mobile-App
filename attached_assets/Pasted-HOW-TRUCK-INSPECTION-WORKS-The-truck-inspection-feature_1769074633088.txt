HOW TRUCK INSPECTION WORKS
The truck inspection feature allows supervisors to visually mark damage on different views of a truck by tapping/clicking on the actual truck images. It's a touch-based, interactive inspection system.

Key Features:
Touch-based damage marking - Tap on truck images to mark damages

Multiple truck views - Front, Back, Driver Side, Passenger Side

Damage type classification - Different colors for different damage types:
