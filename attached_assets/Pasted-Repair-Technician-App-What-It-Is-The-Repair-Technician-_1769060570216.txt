Repair Technician App
What It Is
The Repair Technician App is a job management and estimating tool for repair technicians who handle equipment repairs, create estimates/quotes, and complete assigned repair jobs at commercial pool properties.

Who Uses It
Repair Technicians - Field workers who:

Diagnose and fix pool equipment (pumps, heaters, filters)
Create repair estimates for customer approval
Complete approved repair jobs
Track parts and labor
