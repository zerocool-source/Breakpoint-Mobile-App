Supervisor App
What It Is
The Supervisor App is a team management and oversight tool for field supervisors who oversee multiple service and repair technicians. It provides real-time visibility into team activities, allows task assignments, and enables quality control inspections.

Who Uses It
Supervisors - Office or field managers responsible for:

Monitoring technician progress throughout the day
Assigning special tasks to technicians
Communicating with the team
Performing quality control (QC) inspections
Verifying completed work
